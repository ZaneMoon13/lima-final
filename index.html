Bad Example of Game UI Design

Well what's wrong with it? :

As seen in the image above, Destiny 2 struggles with conveying the information neccessary to new players looking at their inventory system to understand the context of the equipment the player is equipping. Stats like Intellect, Discipline, Recovery, have no meaning to a new player when he/she/they first log into the game for the first time. This is a problem. The symbols that may be found on every single piece of equipment have very different meaning behind them, but is trapped behind four different menus that you have to treverse to see the meaning. This is horrible deisgn. A user friendly UI would have a simple mouse over on each stat/symbol/etc. to convey the information quickly instead of forcing players to look online or in the thousands of pages of jargon in the menus. The numbers to the right of every skill also gives no context of what the player is going to be recieving with the equipment. For example: What does +8 in Mobility mean? 10% speed? 10% acceleration? Who knows. That's why it's poor UI design. A good player of a video game wants to know what they are equipping before blindly equipping weapons and armor that may be worse then the gear they currently have.
